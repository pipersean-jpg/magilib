Import from CSV
Add books to your library in bulk by filling in a spreadsheet and uploading it here.
Step 1 — Get the template
Download the template, open it in Excel or Google Sheets, and fill in your books — one per row.
TitleRequired. Everything else is optional. AuthorFirst name then last — e.g. Harry Houdini Artist/SubjectFor biographies — the subject's name Editione.g. First Edition, Revised, Third Year4-digit year only — e.g. 1946 ConditionPoor / Fair / Good / Very Good / Fine / As New Market PriceNumbers only — e.g. 95.00 (no $ sign) Purchase PriceWhat you paid for it Date AddedFormat: YYYY-MM-DD or leave blank Star Rating1–5 or leave blank Sold StatusActive or Sold — leave blank for Active
💡 Delete the 3 example rows before importing. Only the header row and your books should remain.
Step 3 — Upload your file
Save your spreadsheet as a .csv file, then upload it here. Books are added to your existing library — nothing is deleted.

Cloudinary setup (free high-res storage)

    

      
1. Sign up free at cloudinary.com (no credit card)

      
2. From your dashboard, copy your Cloud name

      
3. Go to Settings → Upload → Add upload preset

      
4. Set Signing mode to Unsigned, save, copy the preset name

      
5. Paste both into the fields above

      
Once configured, every new cover photo is automatically stored in full resolution. The catalog still loads fast using the compressed thumbnail, but tapping zoom shows the full-res version.
